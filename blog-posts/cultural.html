Slow down to catch up

In the tech world, everything is fast. Programs are designed to run as fast as possible. Tight deadlines are set so that new software is developed and released quickly. This do everything fast mentality extends all the way down into how tech is taught. Many students in college aren't encouraged to take their time and really learn the craft. They are pushed through the college's program, a program that has had the same structure for the past 30 years, without truly making sure they are ready for the workforce.
